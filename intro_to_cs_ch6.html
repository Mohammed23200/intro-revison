Chapter — Storage

اختبار التخزين (40 اختيار من متعدد + جميع الصح/الخطأ المعطاة)

جاوب وبعدين اضغط عرض النتيجة. الصح باللون الأخضر والخطأ باللون الأحمر. شغال بسلاسة على الكمبيوتر والموبايل + زر همبورجر لإظهار/إخفاء الناف بار.

الأسئلة الاختيارية (40 سؤال)

اختر إجابة واحدة لكل سؤال
1)______ storage is the physical material on which a computer keeps data, instructions, and information.
2)Which of the following is not a secondary storage medium?
3)A(n) ______ device is the computer hardware that records and/or retrieves items to and from storage media.
4)______ disks use magnetic particles to store data, instructions, and information on a disk’s surface.
5)Which is a magnetic storage medium?
6)A(n) ______ disk is a storage device that contains one or more inflexible, circular platters that store data, instructions, and information.
7)Experts estimate that hard disks using perpendicular recording will provide storage capacities about ______ than disks using longitudinal recording.
8)Which of the following is not a characteristic of a hard disk?
9)Hard disks spin at 5,400 to ___ rpm.
10)A ______ occurs when the read/write head of a hard disk touches the surface of a platter.
11)A ______ is a duplicate of a file, program, or disk that can be used in case the original is lost, damaged, or destroyed.
12)A group of two or more integrated hard disks is called a ______.
13)A ______ hard drive enables mobile users easily to transport photos and other files from one computer to another.
14)A disk ______ consists of a special-purpose chip and electronic circuits that control the transfer of data, instructions, and information from a disk to and from the system bus and other components in a computer.
15)Which is not a type of disk controller?
16)Which of the following is not an advantage of SAS over parallel SCSI?
17)All of the following are flash memory devices EXCEPT ______.
18)With a storage capacity of 512 MB to 100 GB, a(n) ___ card is used in digital cameras, smart phones, PDAs, photo printers, portable media players, and notebook and desktop computers.
19)With a storage capacity of 512 MB to 8 GB, a(n) ___ card is used in digital cameras, digital video cameras, smart phones, PDAs, photo printers, and portable media players.
20)With a storage capacity of 256 MB to 2 GB, a(n) ___ is used in digital cameras and photo printers.
21)With a storage capacity of 1 to 16 GB, a(n) ___ is used in digital cameras, smart phones, and handheld game consoles.
22)A card ___ is a device that reads and writes data, instructions, and information stored on memory cards.
23)Current USB flash drives have storage capacities ranging from ___.
24)___ storage is an Internet service that provides hard disk storage to computer users.
25)Some users choose ___ storage instead of storing data locally on a hard disk or other media.
26)A(n) ___ disc is a type of optical storage media that consists of a flat, round, portable disc made of metal, plastic, and lacquer.
27)A ___ disc can be read, written to, and erased.
28)A typical CD-ROM holds up to ___ of data.
29)A Kodak ___ CD stores digital versions of film using a jpg file format.
30)A newer, quite expensive DVD format is ___, which has storage capacities of 100 GB, with expectations of exceeding 200 GB in the future.
31)A ___ is a high-capacity optical disc that users can read but not write on or erase.
32)___ is a DVD format that potentially will contain up to 20 layers, each with a capacity of 5 GB.
33)As pictured, ____ is a magnetically coated ribbon of plastic capable of storing large amounts of data and information at a low cost.
34)A tape ____ reads and writes data and information on a tape.
35)A(n) ____ stores data on a thin microprocessor embedded in the card.
36)____ is a 100- to 215-foot roll of film used to store microscopic images of documents.
37)____ is a small sheet of film, usually about 4 x 6 inches, used to store microscopic images of documents.
38)Which has the longest life expectancy?
39)A large business, commonly referred to as a(n)____, has hundreds or thousands of employees.
40)In an enterprise, some storage systems can provide more than ____ of storage capacity.
