أسئلة الصح والخطأ (25 سؤال)

اختر (T) صح أو (F) خطأ
1)The case of the system unit is made of metal or plastic and protects the internal electronic components from damage.
2)Each processor on a multi-core processor chip generally runs at a faster clock speed than a single-core processor.
3)The arithmetic logic unit (ALU) is the component of the processor that directs and coordinates most of the operations in a computer.
4)The slower the clock speed, the more instructions a processor can execute per second.
5)Few high-performance PCs use processors from the Intel Core family.
6)A computer uses the binary system in which the digit 0 represents the electronic state of on, and the digit 1 represents the electronic state of off.
7)The ASCII scheme is used primarily on mainframe computers and high-end servers.
8)Coding schemes such as ASCII make it possible for humans to interact with a digital computer that processes only bits.
9)Manufacturers state the size of memory chips and storage devices in terms of the number of bytes the chip or device has available for storage.
10)A megabyte (MB) is equal to approximately 1 billion bytes.
11)Nonvolatile memory loses its contents when the power is removed from a computer.
12)When a computer’s power is turned off, volatile memory does not lose its contents.
13)RAM can hold multiple programs simultaneously, provided the computer has enough RAM to accommodate all the programs.
14)MRAM chips must be re-energized constantly or they lose their contents.
15)Static RAM (SRAM) chips are slower and less reliable than any variation of DRAM chips and must be re-energized more often.
16)Although access times of memory have little effect on overall computer performance, manufacturers usually list a computer’s memory in terms of its access time, not its size.
17)Some motherboards include all necessary capabilities and do not require adapter cards.
18)A bay is the same as a slot, and is used for the installation of adapter cards.
19)On a notebook computer, the ports can be located on the back, front, and/or sides.
20)Personal computers typically have one or two ports on the front and/or back of the system unit.
21)Like IrDA, Bluetooth devices have to be aligned with each other.
22)A port replicator is an external device that attaches to a mobile computer or device and contains a power connection and provides connections to peripherals.
23)The lower the bus clock speed, the faster the transmission of data, which results in applications running faster.
24)An external drive bay is concealed entirely within the system unit.
25)Cleaning a computer once or twice a year can extend its life.
